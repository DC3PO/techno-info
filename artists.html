Artists

Find out who is thumping in the world of techno.

Adam Beyer

Drumcode Radio

When breaking down doors for techno, Drumcode Radio is at the forfront of the assult. With their weekly podcast being released every Thursday it's the best way to power through the rest of the week and into the weekend for the serious dark warriors.

About Adam

Hailing from Sweden, Adam has been in the techno scene since the early 90's and first brought this wonderful music to the United States. From being a raver to throwing down some of the best sets you will ever hear, Adam lives, eats, and breaths techno. If you are curious about the dark side, give Drumcode Radio a listen or if your lucky enough, catch him live, it will bring you over, I promise.

  • @realAdamBeyer
  • Drumcode Radio SoundCloud

Ida Engberg

Techno Dj and Super Mom

Ida Engberg, also from Sweden, has been in the scene quite sometime herself, being one of the many techno queens paving the way for women in the industry. She has played at all the top cubs, by herself and alongside husband Adam Beyer.

About Ida

When shes not dropping techno bombs, she is taking care of her kids and being a supportive wife to Adam. You could say the are THE techno power couple. We will only see what the future holds for her and her family.

  • @IdaEngberg
  • Ida Engberg SoundCloud

Sven Vath

Papa Sven

If you like techno and haven't heard of Sven Vath, then you have been living uder a rock. Sven has played techno all over the world and still continues today. If you EVER get the chance to see him, do NOT pass it up.

About Sven

Probabaly has the longest running career in the techno scene, Sven Vath has been dropping bombs and wooing crowds for over 30 years. Origianlly from Germny, Sven has owned famous night clubs, started his own company Cocoon, which even has its own record label and booking agency.

  • @svenvaeth
  • Cocoon Profile

Paul Ritch

From Paris with Love

France has many great electronic artist but the one who has put his foot in techno, is Paul Ritch. Playing minimal techno, he keeps the crowd grooving for as long as he pleases with his sets lasting up to and past 8 hours.

About Paul

Obvisouly from Paris, Paul Ritch has been pumping out techno since the mid 2000s. Rising to inernationl recognition in 2007, Paul continuousy has new proudction coming out releasing on many different record labels, even his own. His sets will put you in a state of trance that is so dark and calming, once it's over you will be in a different state of mind.
